Frequently Asked Questions

What is SubKiller?

SubKiller is a simple tool that helps you see how much your subscriptions cost per month and per year. It helps make recurring expenses easier to understand.

Does SubKiller cancel subscriptions for me?

No. SubKiller does not cancel subscriptions automatically. It helps you track your recurring costs so you can decide what to keep, cancel, or review.

Is my data saved?

Your subscriptions are saved locally in your browser if local storage is enabled. This means the data stays on your device and is not sent to a server.

Do I need an account?

No. SubKiller works without an account, login, or sign-up.

Why should I track yearly costs?

Small monthly payments can look harmless, but they can become expensive over a full year. Seeing the yearly total makes the real cost easier to judge.

Can I use SubKiller for free?

Yes. The current version of SubKiller is free to use.

Who is SubKiller for?

SubKiller is useful for anyone who wants a clearer overview of subscriptions, memberships, apps, streaming services, software tools, and other recurring payments.

Smart Subscription Tips

  • Review your subscriptions once per month.
  • Cancel services you have not used in the last 30 to 60 days.
  • Be careful with free trials that turn into paid plans.
  • Track yearly subscriptions, not only monthly ones.
  • Separate must-have subscriptions from nice-to-have subscriptions.
  • Set a reminder before expensive renewals.
  • Look at the total yearly cost before deciding if something is worth keeping.

SubKiller provides general information to help you understand your recurring costs. It is not financial advice. You are responsible for your own financial decisions.
